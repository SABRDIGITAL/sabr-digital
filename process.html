THE PROCESS

How we go from “no website” to live in a few steps

We’re not trying to drown you in tech talk. Our process is built so you always know what’s happening and what comes next.

01

Quick intro chat

We jump on a quick call or WhatsApp chat to understand your business, your services and what you want your website to do.

  • • Who you are and where you’re based.
  • • What you offer and who your ideal customer is.
  • • Any existing website or social links you already have.
02

Send us the basics

You send logo (if you have one), photos, key info and anything you definitely want on the site. If you don’t have good photos yet, we can work with simple placeholders first.

  • • Your logo and brand colours (if you have them).
  • • A few photos of your shop/workspace or projects.
  • • Your prices or a rough idea of your services.
03

We design your site

We build a clean, modern draft of your website using the SABR layout system, tailored to your business type (barber, tattoo, trades etc).

  • • Homepage focused on trust, services and clear action.
  • • Work/portfolio section to show real results.
  • • Simple contact options that suit how you actually work.
04

You give feedback

Once the first version is ready, we send you a link. You tell us what you like, what feels off and what needs changing.

  • • Tweak wording, colours or photos.
  • • Add or remove sections based on your priorities.
  • • Adjust anything that doesn’t feel like “you” yet.
05

Go live & handover

We connect your domain, double-check everything on mobile and desktop, then officially launch your new website.

  • • Connect your chosen domain (e.g. yourbusiness.co.uk).
  • • Basic SEO settings so Google can index the site.
  • • Simple handover so you know how to request updates.

Want us to keep the site updated?

If you don’t want to touch the tech side at all, we can look after updates for you – adding new photos, tweaking prices and keeping things running smoothly.
