Three example builds

These are concept projects designed to show what your new website could look like. Once you’ve launched real client sites, we’ll swap these for live projects.

01 · Barber Studio

Fade Room Barbers – clean, modern studio website

Barber studio website screenshot placeholder

We designed a sharp, dark-mode site that matches the studio vibe – strong photos, clear services list and a simple booking flow.

  • Hero section that shows the studio’s personality at a glance.
  • Service list with simple pricing and clear “book now” call-to-action.
  • Gallery of cuts and beards to showcase real results.

02 · Tattoo Studio

Blackline Ink – dark, bold portfolio website

Tattoo studio website screenshot placeholder

A dark, high-contrast site built around the artwork – each artist has their own section with style tags and a direct enquiry button.

  • Grid of tattoo photos with hover states and style labels.
  • Artist profiles with their own mini portfolios.
  • Simple enquiry form that sends all the info the studio needs.

03 · Trades & Construction

Oak & Iron Carpentry – trusted local trades website

Trades & construction website screenshot placeholder

A straight-to-the-point site focused on trust, before/after photos and clear services – built to help win more quotes at better prices.
